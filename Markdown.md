# Formatting

`Normal`  = Normal

 `**Bold**`  = **Bold**
 `__Bold__`  = __Bold__

`*Italic*` = *Italic*
`_Italic_` = _Italic_

`***Bold & Italics***` = ***Bold & Italics***
`___Bold & Italics___` = ___Bold & Italics___

`Subscript example: H~2~O` = H~2~O

`Superscript example: X^2^` = X^2^

`==Highlight==` = ==Highlight==

`~~Strikethrough~~` = ~~Strikethrough~~
